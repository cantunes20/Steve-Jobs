Sidebar

Steve Jobs

"The ones who are crazy enough to think they can change the world, are the ones who do." -Steve Jobs

Before He was Famous

Steve Jobs once said, “We started out to get a computer in the hands of everyday people, and we succeeded beyond our wildest dreams." Before he was a famous man known for being the cofounder and CEO of Apple Inc, Jobs was aimlessly going through life. He was born on February 24, 1955 in San Francisco. Jobs started his life playing around with electronics in his home. Later, he attended Reed College; however, he dropped out and went to the classes for free. Steve Jobs lived in India for a short time, and when he returned home, he and his friend, Steve Wozniak, started the Apple Company.

Apple Inc.

"If today were the last day of your life, would you want to do what you are about to do today?" -Steve Jobs

In 1977, the Apple Company become big. It introduced the Apple II computer, and two years after the first sale of the computer, the sales had become 200 million dollars. However, within a couple of years, the Apple sales started to decline, and Steve Jobs introduced computers that did not satisfy the customers. Because of this, the CEO that Jobs hired expelled him from the board. Two years later, he left Apple, sold his stocks, and put all his focus on his company, Pixar Animation Studios. Even without Apple, Jobs continued to do great things. He created a computer animated film that became a hit and made his share worth one billion dollars.

Bringing Back Steve Jobs

The sales of Apple by the late 1990’s had plummeted, so the company took back Jobs, hoping for him to help the company’s revenue. Because of Jobs, the Apple company became the face of innovation. The sales continued to climb, along with the inventions that Apple made. For years, Apple continued to be incredibly successful under the guidance of Steve Jobs.

Later Years

Steve Jobs, though immensely successful, had health issues. In late 2011, he resigned as CEO, and a couple of months later, he died. Steve Jobs went from years of struggling to make ends me, to being one of the most successful businessmen. It is amazing how life changed so much for him throughout the years, and it is amazing how one person can accomplish.
