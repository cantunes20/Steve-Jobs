Where is Apple Inc. now?

Apple is now the worlds largest and most valuable IT company by revenue. As of 2015, the company was selling about 800,000 iPhones a day. It is worth almost $500 billion.

Fun Facts about Apple

  1. Apple has more operating cash than the U.S. Treasury.
  2. A person has a better chance of getting into Harvard University than getting a job at the Apple Store.
  3. In 2015, Apple Inc. was worth more than Switzerland, Sweden or Nigeria.
  4. Apple could buy Disney or Coca-Cola and pay in cash.
